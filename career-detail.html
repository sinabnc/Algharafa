Civil Engineer

Job Category: Engineer

Job Type: Full Time

Job Location: Doha

A leading international engineering practice, offering services in architecture, interior design, master planning, landscape, urban design, and building consultancy within Asia & Middle East, currently requires a Senior Civil Engineer to join their organization.

Qualification:

  • Minimum Bachelor’s Degree in Civil Engineering or relevant subject
  • Education qualification obtained from North America, West Europe, Australia
  • Holder of Chartered Membership in relevant discipline (e.g., Engineering) would be an advantage
  • MBA and/or secondary relevant degree would be advantageous
  • Holder of current/valid Driving License

Experience:

  • 15 years of cumulative experience in a relevant field, minimum 5 years in treated/waste water including managing contractors and leading the team
  • Experience of working in a multidisciplinary organization preferably in the Middle East
  • Knowledge and experience in Contract Management. Ability to deliver and support change
  • Knowledge of Quality, Health and Safety, and Environment policies, procedures, and regulations
  • Competent in Microsoft Office Packages
  • Ability to influence and engage stakeholders and staff at all levels within the organization
  • Ability to communicate verbally and in writing in English is mandatory
  • Ability to communicate verbally and in writing in Arabic is preferred

If you fit the criteria, please feel free to apply.
